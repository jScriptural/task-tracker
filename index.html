Task Tracker

Task Tracker is a simple and easy-to-use todo app that helps you stay organise and productive. Task Tracker empowers you to regain control over your busy life, ensuring that you never miss a deadline or forget an essential task.

    Features:
  • Simple and user-friendly interface
  • Create and delete tasks.
  • Set priorities and due dates.
  • Organise tasks into categories for seamless filtering.
  • Search for task by category or unique id.
